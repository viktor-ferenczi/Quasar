# Quasar.Models — Domain Models

*Module `Quasar.Models` — 15 files.* See the [handbook TOC](../TOC.md) and the [file Index](../Index.md).

Plain domain-model layer with no behaviour or DI. It centres on the managed DS instance: the persisted `DedicatedServerDefinition` (including its per-server `CpuAffinity` cpuset, parsed/validated by the static `CpuAffinitySpec` helper, and its `ManagedRuntime` Magnetar-build selector), the volatile `DedicatedServerRuntimeSnapshot`, and the lifecycle enums (goal / process / health state, process priority, and managed runtime). It also defines the config-profile model (`QuasarConfigProfile`, covering world-root and ~90 session settings plus plugins/mods), world templates, branding settings and palette, known-player records, and the configuration-backup models (`QuasarBackupManifest`, `QuasarBackupSettings`/`BackupFrequency`, `QuasarRestoreReport`). These types are produced and consumed throughout [Quasar.Services.Core](Quasar.Services.Core.md), [Quasar.Services.Backup](Quasar.Services.Backup.md) and the [UI](Quasar.Components.md).
